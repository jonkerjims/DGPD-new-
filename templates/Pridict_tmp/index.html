{# Introduction #}
Introduction

Dense granule proteins (GRAs) are a category of immunocompetent protein secreted by Apicomplexa protozoa, which have diverse functions secreted by different protozoa. Many studies have proved that GRAs play a crucial role in causing neuromuscular dysfunction in cattle, dogs, etc. and reproductive disorders in pregnant animals. Therefore, the identification of GRAs is significant for the study on prevention and treatment of parasitic diseases.

Considering that biological experiment approach is time-consuming and labor-intensive, computational method is a superior choice. To our knowledge, this is the first attempt to utilize computational approach for GRAs prediction task. In this work, we implemented the graph convolution operation to accomplish the prediction work.
